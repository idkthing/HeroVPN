How HeroVPN works

    HeroVPN is a fully in-website proxy, when we recieve your request our servers go to work.

  • 1. When we recieve your request, first clean and analize the URL. Once our team of expert algorithms determines the URL is safe, we verify you are a human.
  • 2. The our server spins up a request to your desired website and voila, our servers can now prepare it for you!
  • 3. We then search through the website preparing all third-party resources to be redirected to us. This step ensures your browser can't send any naughty requests to track you.
  • 4. After those steps we can finally send all that data over to you with Secure Hypertext Transfer Protocol so that you can go about your day with peace of mind.
